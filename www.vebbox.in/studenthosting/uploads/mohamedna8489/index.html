AI Development

AI development involves researching and refining algorithms to enable machines to perform tasks traditionally requiring human intelligence. This process includes collecting and preprocessing data, training models, evaluating performance, and deploying them into real-world applications. Continuous improvement is integral, with iterative refinement based on feedback and evolving requirements.

Snowflake

Snowflake is a popular cloud-based data warehousing platform known for its scalability, flexibility, and ease of use. It allows businesses to store and analyze large volumes of data from multiple sources efficiently. Snowflake's architecture separates storage and computing, enabling seamless scalability and cost-effectiveness.

Oracle cloud

Oracle Cloud is a cloud computing service offered by Oracle Corporation. It provides a wide range of cloud-based services including infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and software as a service (SaaS) solutions. Oracle Cloud allows businesses to build, deploy, and manage applications and services in the cloud, providing scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness.

ForgeRock

ForgeRock is a leading provider of digital identity management solutions. The company offers a comprehensive platform that enables organizations to securely manage digital identities, access controls, and user permissions across various applications, devices, and services. ForgeRock's platform helps businesses enhance security, protect customer data, and improve user experiences by providing a seamless and secure way to manage identities and access.

AEM

Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) is a comprehensive content management solution for building websites, mobile apps, and forms. It allows organizations to create, manage, and deliver personalized digital experiences across multiple channels and devices. AEM provides tools for managing digital assets, creating and editing web content, and optimizing the user experience.

Azure

Azure provides organizations with the flexibility to build, deploy, and manage applications and services on a global scale. It supports various programming languages, frameworks, and tools, making it suitable for a wide range of use cases, from web applications to big data processing.

How we
WORK

We work differently and were proud of it. Our teams are empowered to solve the most urgent and relevant problems they see for our customers. We all share the responsibility of making Wise a success. We empower Wisers to make decisions and take ownership of how they work best. Teams and individuals have different needs thats why we have company-wide principles, and then our teams set their own guidelines.

We work in empowered teams

Empowered teams are central to the way we work. In our product and engineering teams for example youre a key part of your team, coordinating your vision alongside product managers, engineers, analysts, designers, legal specialists and operational teams to name a few. Helping to decide what to prioritise and deliver. Working this way helps us move at speed and achieve our mission faster. In customer support youll have the trust to solve complex problems, as were working smarter to address customer issues in the most effective way. You'll have a lead and a team to support you, so theres always someone to talk things through with and tackle challenges.

We put our customers first

Were working to create a fairer financial system for real people. So we make sure our customers are at the centre of everything we do. Whether thats working through feedback, inviting customers to test our product, or having them talk us through how were doing. We listen so that we can understand their needs and identify how we can keep improving our products and services. Each teams success is defined by the positive impact theyve made for our customers, and each team has KPIs (key performance indicators) that let them know how well theyre doing. We should all be able to stand up in front of our company and explain how our work helps our customers.

We're flexible

We want to empower Wisers to make decisions and take ownership of how they work best. We believe that if you are intentional about where and who you work with, youll be able to get the best out of any working environment. We know that teams and individuals all have different needs depending on what they need to deliver, as well as their personal circumstances. Thats why we have company-wide principles around flexible working and our teams set their own guidelines too. We expect that new joiners will be in the office most days to build connections and learn from colleagues for their first six months."
